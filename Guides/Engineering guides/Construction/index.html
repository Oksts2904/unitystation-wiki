Construction¶

Construction and deconstruction are essential tools for Engineers and Traitors alike,

The main feature of space station 13 is for any item in the game to be either ordered from the quartermaster, or built with materials.

As this game is a work-in-progress, we're not quite there yet, here are some things you can build and deconstruct, though.

### Normal Wall

Just a basic wall, they are easy to build and destroy, and an easy way to keep out the cold of space.

How to Build Requres to Build
x4 1. Stand where you want the wall.
2. Click the metal plates* in your hand to open the construction panel.
3. Choose 'Build wall girders' from the list.
Use the remaining 2 sheets of metal plates on the girder.

False wall*

1. Stand where you want the wall.
2. Click the metal in your hand to open the construction panel.
3. Choose 'Build wall girders' from the list.
4. Unsecure the girder you built with a wrench
5. click on the girder with at least 2 metal plates**
How to deconstruct
Tools




1. Weld off the plating.
2. Use a Wrench to unsecure the girder.
3.Screwdriver to disassemble the girder.

Reinforced Wall¶

A more hardy sort of wall, these take significantly longer to deconstruct, but are still fairly easy to place.

You'll find them lining command, security, and anywhere the Captain doesn't want you in.

Reinforced Wall How to Build Requres to Build
x2 x2 1. Stand where you want the reinforced wall.
2. Click the metal in your hand to open the construction panel.
3. Choose 'Build girders' form the list.
4. Use the plasteel on the girders to reinforce them.
5. Use the last plasteel sheet on the reinforced girders to finish the wall.
6. False Reinforced wallStand where you want the reinforced wall.
7. Click the metal in your hand to open the construction panel.
8. Choose 'Build girder' form the list.Unsecure the girder with a Wrench
9. Use 1plasteel on the girders to reinforce them.
10. Use another plastee sheet on the reinforced girders to finish the wall.
How to deconstruct
Tools 1. Wirecutters to cut the outer grille.
2. Screwdriver to remove the support lines.
3. Weld through the metal cover.
4. Crowbar to pry off the cover.
5. Wrench to loosen the support rods.
6. Weld through the support rods.
7. Crowbar to pry off the outer sheath.
8. Screwdriver to unsecure the support struts.
9. Wirecutters to remove the support struts.
10. Wrench to unsecure the girder.
11. Screwdriver to disassemble it.		 l




Computer Frame¶

used to create all of the Computers on the Station

How to Build Requres to Build
x5 1. Hold at least 5 Metal plate in your hand
2. Press on the metal plates in your hand
3. Select the Computer Frame icon
4. use wrench to secure
5. insert a chip of your choice by clicking on the secured frame with the chip
6. click on the frame with a Screwdriver to screw into place
7. Click on the frame with 5 Medium power cables
8. click on the frame with 2 glass sheets
9. Click on the frame with a screwdriver to connect the monitor
How to deconstruct
Tools 1. Click on the frame with a screwdriver to disconnect the monitor
2. Click on the frame with a crowbar to remove glass panel
3. Click on the frame with wirecutter to remove Medium power cables
4.Click on the frame with screwdriver to unscrew the chip
5. wrench the frame to unsecure
6. Click on unsecured frame with a Crowbar to remove chip
7. Click on the frame with a lit Welding Tool to deconstruct

Reinforced Window¶

Like a window, but stronger.

Required to Build Requres to Build
x2 1. Hold at least 2 reinforced glass sheets in your hand
2. Press on the reinforced glass in your hand
3. Select the window icon.
4. Pull and drag the window to the place you want it installed.
4. Click on the window with a screw driver in hand to anchor it in place.
5. Use a crowbar to fully secure the reinforced window.
How to Deconstruct
Tools 1. Use a lit welder on harm intent to heat up the one-way screws.
2. Use a screwdriver to remove screws .
3. Use a crowbar to pry the window out of its frame.
4. Use wire cutters to cut exposed bars on the window.
5. Use a wrench to unbolt and un-anchor the window .
6. Use a wrench again to deconstruct the window into sheets.
